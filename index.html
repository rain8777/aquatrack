Why I Made AquaTrack

Water refilling stations are one of the most common small businesses in the Philippines. Yet most of them still rely on paper records, text message orders, and verbal communication for deliveries. This leads to missed orders, confused delivery routes, and unhappy customers.

I wanted to give small business owners — especially those with little to no tech experience — a tool they could actually use. No complicated software. No confusing menus. Just open it on any device, see your customers on a map, manage your delivery queue, and get more water delivered every day.

AquaTrack was built with love for Filipino water refilling station owners everywhere. My goal is simple: help you spend less time managing and more time growing your business.
